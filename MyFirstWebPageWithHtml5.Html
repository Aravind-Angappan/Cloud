Steps Involved in Fixing Appointment and getting Police Clearance Certificate(PCC):

A passport is a travel document, usually issued by a country's government to its citizens, that certifies the identity and nationality of its holder primarily for the purpose of international travel.Standard passports may contain information such as the holder's name, place and date of birth, photograph, signature, and other relevant identifying information.

  1. First you need to register yourself in Passport Kendra Seva with email ID and a password.
  2. Then, you need to login with the given ID and password.Click on apply for Passport link.
  3. Then you would be asked to provide details.Make sure you give your details that is consistent with government records
  4. After submitting the details,you would be given a link to fix an appointment.

    Click here to know more

  5. After fixing appointment,pay the charged amount(amount can be paid throgh net banking),print the receipt and fix an appointment for PCC.
  6. The police would visit your home and verify your details
  7. You would then be provided with passport

Documents to be submitted

  1. Birth Proof(Birth Certificate,PAN Card,Aadhar Card
  2. Address Proof(Aadhar Card,Voter ID)
  3. Degree/Metric Certificate(Should have a Birth date on said Certificate[For Non-ECR Category])
  4. Passport Size Photo
Note:You cannot have aadhar card for both address proof and Birth proof simultaneously.Have different document for both proof.

Selecting your Passport Office:

After finishing your application,you need to select your passport office or post office to verify your document.

First, you would be (again) asked to provide details.After providing them you need to select your neraest passport office which would be provided based on RPO selected by you.

The available passport/post office will be provided in the table like the one given below.

S.No Passport/Post Office Date
1 POPSK-Tirunelveli 03/02/2021
2 POPSK-Thoothukudi 04/02/2021
3 POPSK-Madurai 05/02/2021
4 Post Office PSK-Tirunelveli 04/02/2021

Depending on your place and time,choose one of them and finish the verification of your documents.(Based on RPO-Madurai)

Note:

They wil allot a time slot at which you must finsh your verification.After this,they will dispatch a police to again verify your details to your home and give you a PCC(Police Clearance Certificate).So make sure you have given correct address.
